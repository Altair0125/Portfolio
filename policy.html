Private Policy

Privacy Policy for Singapore Delights At Singapore Delights, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard it. Information We Collect: Contact Information: We may collect your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you place an order or sign up for promotions or newsletters. Payment Information: When you make a purchase, we collect payment information such as credit card details or other payment methods to process your transaction securely. Order History: We may retain records of your past purchases and preferences to provide personalized recommendations and improve our services. Device Information: When you interact with our website or mobile app, we may collect information about your device, including IP address, browser type, and operating system, to enhance your browsing experience and prevent fraudulent activity. How We Use Your Information: Order Fulfillment: We use your contact and payment information to process your orders, deliver products, and provide customer support. Marketing and Promotions: With your consent, we may use your contact information to send you promotional offers, updates, and newsletters about our products and services. Analytics: We may analyze user data to understand customer preferences, improve our products, and optimize the performance of our website and mobile app. Data Security: Encryption: We utilize industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your personal information during transmission and storage. Access Controls: Access to your personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only, and we implement strict access controls to prevent unauthorized access or misuse. Third-Party Services: We may use third-party service providers to assist with payment processing, analytics, and marketing. These providers are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information. Your Choices: Opt-Out: You may opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the instructions provided in our emails or contacting customer support. Access and Correction: You have the right to access, update, or correct your personal information. If you would like to request a copy of your data or make changes, please contact us using the information provided below. Updates to this Privacy Policy: We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this policy regularly for any updates. Contact Us: If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact us at privacy@singaporedelights.com.
